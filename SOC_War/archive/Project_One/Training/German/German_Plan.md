# 🇩🇪 German Learning Protocol – June 2025

## 🗓 Weekly Structure
| Week | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Week 1 | Basics: Greetings, Cafe Orders, Family |
| Week 2 | Numbers, Colors, Professions |
| Week 3 | Places, Travel, Directions |
| Week 4 | Grammar: Verb conjugation, Sentence building |

---

## 🧠 Daily Practice Routine
- 🎯 Duolingo (15–20 mins minimum)
- ✍️ Log new vocab daily to `Logs/German/YYYYMMDD.md`
- 🗣️ Speak 3–5 German sentences out loud each night
- 🧾 Listen to 1 German audio sentence (e.g., Easy German YT)

---

## 📚 Resources
- [Duolingo German](https://www.duolingo.com/course/de/en/Learn-German)
- [Easy German YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/c/easygerman)
- [DW Learn German](https://learngerman.dw.com/en/overview)

---

## 🔁 Weekly Review (every Sunday)
- Rewrite top 10 phrases
- Test recall from earlier logs
- Attempt 3 full sentences with correct gender/article
